The Opportunity

Why Wasl Exists

Talented Muslim founders across Africa often face unique barriers: lack of access to experienced mentors who share their cultural values, minimal practical startup education, and isolated founder environments.

Wasl exists to bridge this gap. We provide a founder-first ecosystem designed to align global venture-building strategies with Islamic ethical values, offering a supportive path to help you build a robust and competitive technology startup.

Ethical Pillars

Our Core Values

We build our community on the foundation of high-character, ethical operation, and collaborative success.

Excellence (Ihsan)

Striving for the highest quality and master craftsmanship in our code, our products, and our daily operations.

Trust (Amanah)

Acting as reliable stewards of founder data, capital, and community connections, upholding our commitments fully.

Integrity

Maintaining absolute honesty and high ethical standards in deal structures, customer acquisition, and team management.

Service

Putting the needs of founders first and paying forward knowledge, access, and resources to uplift the ecosystem.

Learning

Remaining humble and keeping a growth-oriented mindset, continuously adapting to engineering shifts and market needs.

Collaboration

Cooperating across borders, cultures, and fields to create collective impact for African tech ecosystems.
