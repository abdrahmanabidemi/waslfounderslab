Bridging the Gap

Talented Muslim founders across Africa often lack access to experienced mentors, practical startup education, and founder communities. This isolation slows down growth and makes it harder to navigate early-stage venture building.

Wasl exists to solve this problem. We provide a space where high-potential entrepreneurs can connect with global building frameworks while aligning their work with high ethical principles.

"We are building a community where faith, ethics, and world-class technology venture creation work hand-in-hand to solve Africa’s most pressing challenges."
